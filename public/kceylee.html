Framework 02
The Punchy Relatable Observation
i felt my [pop culture character]

— OR —

lowkey why was [specific thing] one of my [superlative] [category] ever

[lowercase funny observation about cat/pet/moment] #relevant

Framework 03
The Curiosity Gap Tease
should i [go back / do it / say yes to] [the thing] tho….

— OR —

the secret [activity/reveal] was…. [surprising specific answer]! [honest reaction]

— OR —

[vague teaser about decision/discovery]…. [minimal context]
Key: genuine incompleteness. Don’t explain too much. Use “….” (4 dots) — it reads as trailing thought. Best for shopping hauls, activity reveals, multi-part content.
